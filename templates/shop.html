Vet Peeves

This field is fun and exciting, but it can absolutely ruffle our feathers. Vet Peeves was created to bring veterinarians and our hardworking staff together, to make light of the day to day stressors of practicing medicine. This career field, while rewarding, can be mentally, physically, and psychologically draining. Sometimes we have to laugh to keep from crying. When the scrubs don't fit like they used to, the otoscope is falling apart, and the barking is slowly driving you insane, it's all we can do to make it through the day. With the increased stress level this career field brings, I, Dr. Qindasi Silva, hope that my game can provide much needed laughs, and a healthy outlet. Enjoy three categories of questions to discuss, joke, and think about. Play with your fellow veterinarians or anyone who understands this field. Tell your stories, discuss your thoughts, and choose the best answers together. Support one another as you vent your frustrations, tell that one story you've been waiting to tell, and know that you are not alone.
